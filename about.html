About Us

Chicken Boss is more than just a chicken restaurant. We are a community of chicken lovers.

In 1524, started in the name of our ancestor, Chicken Boss started this fine establishment at his home in Chicken Valley, Chicken. After over half a millenium, we continue to use the same fine recipies our ancestor used to develop what is still, and always wil be, Chicken Boss.
